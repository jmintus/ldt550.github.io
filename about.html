About Beer 101

Beer 101 is a quick website covering the popular styles of beer found in the market today. While this website is far from comprehensive, the aim is to provide a quick overview of what's available to beer drinkers of all styles. The first iteration of this website will focus solely on ales with a new version diving into the vast world of lagers to follow. Sit back, open a pint, and enjoy.
